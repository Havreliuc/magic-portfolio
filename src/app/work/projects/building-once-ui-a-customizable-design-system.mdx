## Overview

Development of an AI-powered healthcare and patient-care management platform with Angular and PrimeNG, running on AWS and Google Cloud.

## Key Features

- **Patient Care Management**: Built a comprehensive platform for patient profiles, schedules, medications, questionnaires, triggers, tasks, and clinical notes, with role-based access for patients, caregivers, and admins.
- **AI Assistance**: Integrated Google Gemini (via Vertex AI and Genkit) for lab-test/PDF analysis and OCR, AI-driven SMS and WhatsApp replies, scheduling-intent parsing, and AI voice calls through Vapi.
- **Real-time Communication**: Implemented live chat, support rooms, and patient SMS/WhatsApp messaging using Socket.IO and Twilio, including campaign orchestration and message tracking.
- **Async Processing & Triggers**: Designed an event-driven trigger system with BullMQ job queues to automatically generate tasks and notifications based on patient data and events.
- **Documents & E-signature**: Added PDF generation and editing (pdf-lib, jsPDF, Puppeteer), checklist documents, and SignWell e-signature workflows.

## Technologies Used

- **Angular 19 + PrimeNG**: For a modular, reactive, component-rich frontend.
- **NestJS + Prisma**: For a modular TypeScript backend with a type-safe PostgreSQL data layer.
- **Vertex AI (Gemini) + Genkit**: For generative AI, document analysis, and conversational automation.
- **BullMQ + Redis**: For async job queues and scheduled background processing.
- **Twilio & Vapi**: For SMS, voice, video, and AI phone calls.
- **AWS & Google Cloud**: S3, Athena, and Glue alongside Firebase (Auth, Storage, Firestore, FCM), deployed via Docker, Cloud Build, and Kubernetes (GKE).

## Challenges and Learnings

A key challenge was orchestrating many third-party integrations (Twilio, Vapi, SignWell, Garmin, Withings, FHIR) and AI services reliably within a healthcare context where data accuracy and uptime matter. The solution involved a modular service architecture, resilient retry logic for transient network failures, queue-based async processing, and centralized authentication and caching. Coordinating credentials across AWS, Google Cloud, and Firebase — and keeping them secure and consistent across environments — was a valuable operational learning experience.

## Outcome

The platform supports day-to-day patient care for multiple organisations, combining clinical workflows, real-time messaging, and AI automation in a single system. Its modular architecture has made it straightforward to add new features and integrations, while AI assistance has reduced manual work for caregivers and improved responsiveness to patients.
